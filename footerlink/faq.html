Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our website and add your desired products to the cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout to complete your order.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online wallets. Please check the payment options available at checkout.

3. How can I track my order?

After your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on the courier's website.

4. Can I change my order after placing it?

We process orders quickly, but if you need to make any changes, please contact us immediately. We'll do our best to accommodate your request before the order is shipped.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please refer to our shipping page for more details.

6. How do I return an item?

You can return items within 14 days of receipt. Please refer to our return & exchange page for the full policy and process.

7. Can I cancel my order?

Once your order is processed and shipped, it cannot be canceled. However, you can return it after receiving the product. Please refer to our return & exchange page for more information.
