Privacy Policy

At Mushk-by_Huzaifah, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details or other payment methods, which are processed securely by third-party payment providers.
  • Usage Information: Data about your interactions with our website, such as browsing history and products viewed.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional offers (if you have opted in for marketing communications).
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • To prevent fraud and secure your transactions.

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, including SSL encryption to ensure that your information is securely transmitted during transactions.

4. Sharing Your Information

We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners for the following reasons:

  • To process payments and fulfill orders (e.g., payment processors, shipping carriers).
  • For marketing purposes, but only if you have opted in to receive promotional communications.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze website traffic. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the last revision will be updated accordingly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mushkbyhuzaifah.com.
