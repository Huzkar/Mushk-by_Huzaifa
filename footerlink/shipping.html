Shipping Information

At Mushk-by_Huzaifah, we strive to provide a smooth and efficient shipping experience for all our customers. Below, you'll find detailed information regarding our shipping policies.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days. Free for orders over PKR 5000.
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2-3 business days. Additional charges may apply based on location.
  • International Shipping: Available for selected countries. Delivery time varies depending on the destination.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the weight of your order and the shipping method selected. You will see the exact shipping cost during checkout before confirming your order.

3. Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days after your payment has been confirmed. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with a tracking number so you can monitor the progress of your shipment. You can track your order on the courier serviceâ€™s website.

5. Delivery Address

Please ensure that the delivery address is accurate and complete. We are not responsible for any delays or issues caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses. If there is an issue with the address, please contact us immediately at support@mushkbyhuzaifah.com.

6. International Orders

International orders may be subject to additional customs duties and taxes. These charges are determined by the destination country and are the responsibility of the customer. Please check your country's import regulations before placing an order.

7. Damaged or Lost Items

If your item is damaged or lost during shipping, please contact us immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure your satisfaction.

8. Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to PO Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or certain remote areas. Please contact our customer support team if you are unsure whether we can ship to your location.
