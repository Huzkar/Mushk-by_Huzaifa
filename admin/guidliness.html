Admin Guidelines

1. Product Management

  • Add new fragrances with accurate names, prices, and descriptions.
  • Upload high-quality product images.
  • Update or remove discontinued or out-of-stock items.

2. Order Tracking

  • Monitor new orders daily.
  • Update order status promptly (e.g., "Processing", "Shipped").
  • Contact suppliers in case of delivery issues.

3. Customer Feedback

  • Regularly review feedback from the customer feedback page.
  • Respond to negative reviews with professionalism if required.

4. Sales Tracking

  • Use the Manual Sales Tracker to log offline or direct sales.
  • Verify monthly reports for revenue and inventory consistency.

5. Security & Backup

  • Do not share login credentials with unauthorized persons.
  • Back up website data regularly.
  • Log out after completing your tasks.
Note: Only trained admins should access and modify sensitive information. In case of any issue, please report to the development team immediately.
